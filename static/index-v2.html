About Me

The Story Behind The Data

I'm Ayoade Adegbite — an Analytics Engineer, international speaker, and the founder of Behind The Data.

My career started in Mechatronics Engineering in Nigeria, but I quickly discovered that the most impactful problems weren't hardware problems — they were data problems. That pivot changed everything.

Since then, I've worked across Nigeria, Canada, the UK, the US, Poland, and Germany, helping organizations turn messy, undocumented data systems into production-ready analytics platforms. From building a National Nutrition Dashboard for Nigerian healthcare agencies to saving companies $20K annually through automation — I focus on impact you can measure.

"I don't just build systems — I build people. Through academies, bootcamps, and mentorship programs across three continents, I've trained over 900 analysts and engineers."

Currently pursuing my Master's in Information Technology at Miva Open University, and I hold certifications in Tableau, Apache Airflow (Astronomer DAG Authoring), and Scientific Computing with Python.

Tools I've Built

Open-source dbt packages solving real problems in analytics engineering.

dbt-doc-tracker

Detects and records documentation changes across your dbt project. Records only the diffs — essential for audit trails and governance.

dbt-doc-inherit

Propagates column descriptions along the DAG. Define once, inherit downstream. Solves the "documentation drift" problem at scale.
